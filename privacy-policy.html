Vegan Checker Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Vegan Checker ("we", "our", or "us") provides ingredient scanning and classification services designed to help users review food ingredient labels. This Privacy Policy explains how information is processed when you use the Vegan Checker mobile application.

Information We Process

When using Vegan Checker, you may choose to scan ingredient labels using your device camera or photo library.

The application may process:

  • Images of food ingredient labels
  • Ingredient text extracted from scanned labels
  • Classification results generated from ingredient analysis
  • Application diagnostic information necessary to maintain and improve app functionality

How Information Is Used

Information is processed solely for the purpose of:

  • Extracting ingredient information from food labels
  • Classifying ingredients as vegan, non-vegan, or requiring further review
  • Providing scan history and application features
  • Improving application reliability and performance

Artificial Intelligence Processing

Ingredient text extracted from food labels may be processed using third-party artificial intelligence services to generate ingredient classifications and related analysis.

These services are used only to provide Vegan Checker functionality.

Photo and Camera Access

Camera and photo library access are used only when you choose to scan or import ingredient labels.

Images are used to analyze ingredient information and generate scan results.

Data Storage

Scan history may be stored locally on your device to improve your experience and allow access to previous scans.

We do not guarantee permanent retention of scan history.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Information may be processed by trusted service providers solely to operate and improve Vegan Checker functionality.

Third-Party Services

Vegan Checker may utilize third-party service providers for application functionality, analytics, error reporting, infrastructure, OCR processing, artificial intelligence processing, or related services.

These providers may process information only as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

Children's Privacy

Vegan Checker is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Disclaimer

Vegan Checker is provided for informational and educational purposes only.

Ingredient classifications are generated using automated systems and may not be accurate in all situations.

Users should independently verify ingredient labels, manufacturer information, allergen disclosures, certifications, and dietary requirements before making purchasing or consumption decisions.

Vegan Checker does not provide medical, nutritional, allergy, legal, or certification advice.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
